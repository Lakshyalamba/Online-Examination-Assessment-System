# Landing Page Inspiration Prompt

## Page Purpose

Present the project as a serious academic examination platform with a clear system-design identity and a believable product overview.

## Key Visual Hierarchy

- strong hero headline and concise value summary
- product preview panel or dashboard collage
- role cards for Admin, Examiner, Student
- feature grid for question bank, timed exams, grading, results, analytics, auditability
- workflow strip showing create, attempt, review, publish

## Target Aesthetic

Modern academic SaaS, light-theme first, polished but restrained, trustworthy and operational rather than flashy.

## Important Sections And Components

- header with project title and sign-in CTA
- hero area with strong typography
- preview card stack showing dashboard motifs
- benefits grid
- footer with project and team context

## Interaction Feel

Calm, credible, clearly structured, with emphasis on navigation clarity and confidence.

## Layout Constraints

- 1440px desktop-friendly composition
- maintain generous whitespace without becoming empty
- avoid marketing gradients or oversized 3D illustration styles

## Consistency Rules From `design.md`

- navy for structural framing
- teal for primary CTA and active highlights
- light slate surfaces, soft borders, rounded cards
- `Manrope` style headline energy with readable academic tone
