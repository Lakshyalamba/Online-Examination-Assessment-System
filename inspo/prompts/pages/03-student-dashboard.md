# Student Dashboard Inspiration Prompt

## Page Purpose

Show a student exactly what needs attention now: upcoming exams, active attempts, and published results.

## Key Visual Hierarchy

- next exam hero or urgent alert panel
- quick metric cards
- assigned exams list
- recent results summary
- instruction or announcement card

## Target Aesthetic

Focused, readable, slightly calmer than examiner or admin dashboards, with clear urgency only where time matters.

## Important Sections And Components

- sidebar shell
- top header with student greeting
- upcoming exams card
- active exam or deadline strip
- recent results and notices

## Interaction Feel

Supportive and task-oriented, with one obvious next action at a time.

## Layout Constraints

- dashboard should feel usable for students who open it briefly before an exam
- do not overcrowd analytics or admin-style panels

## Consistency Rules From `design.md`

- status chips are prominent
- card layout with generous spacing
- amber only for time-sensitive alerts
- teal for start or continue actions
