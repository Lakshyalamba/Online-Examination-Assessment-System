# Login Page Inspiration Prompt

## Page Purpose

Create a secure, calm sign-in screen that feels appropriate for academic operations and timed assessments.

## Key Visual Hierarchy

- focused login card
- clear form labels and button hierarchy
- small trust or security side panel
- supporting password or account-help link

## Target Aesthetic

Minimal, high-trust, calm, and institution-friendly.

## Important Sections And Components

- sign-in form with email and password
- primary submit button
- subtle security or exam-integrity note
- optional invite activation or forgot-password secondary link

## Interaction Feel

Quiet, direct, low-distraction, and accessible.

## Layout Constraints

- use a centered or split-panel layout
- keep the form compact and readable
- avoid overly decorative onboarding patterns

## Consistency Rules From `design.md`

- white card on soft sky background
- teal focus states on inputs
- navy heading and muted explanatory text
- clear spacing and strong contrast
