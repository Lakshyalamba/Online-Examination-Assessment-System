# Grading And Review Page Inspiration Prompt

## Page Purpose

Support fast and auditable subjective answer review without visual confusion.

## Key Visual Hierarchy

- review queue context
- question and rubric area
- student answer panel
- marks and feedback controls
- navigation between submissions

## Target Aesthetic

Professional, text-friendly, and quietly analytical.

## Important Sections And Components

- split-screen grading workspace
- student answer viewer
- marks entry panel
- reviewer notes area
- progress through pending review items

## Interaction Feel

Focused and precise, optimized for repetitive marking work.

## Layout Constraints

- keep long text areas readable
- preserve clear separation between source answer and scoring controls

## Consistency Rules From `design.md`

- use neutral surfaces with minimal decoration
- keep action controls pinned or visually stable
- highlight review status with consistent badges
