# Analytics And Report Page Inspiration Prompt

## Page Purpose

Show exam and operational trends clearly enough for faculty and reviewers to interpret quickly.

## Key Visual Hierarchy

- KPI summary row
- trend and distribution charts
- filter bar
- supporting detail table

## Target Aesthetic

Data-clear and credible, with restrained visual energy.

## Important Sections And Components

- exam or date selector
- metric cards
- bar, line, or distribution charts
- question-level detail table or summary list

## Interaction Feel

Analytical and calm, designed for interpretation rather than exploration-heavy BI behavior.

## Layout Constraints

- chart colors should remain limited and semantically consistent
- maintain strong spacing between chart cards and tables

## Consistency Rules From `design.md`

- use muted chart palette with navy and teal anchors
- chart cards should match dashboard cards
- filters should align with table-toolbar language used elsewhere

Avoid: rainbow charts, data-viz overload, glossy dark dashboard, decorative background effects
