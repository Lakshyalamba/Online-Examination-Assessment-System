# Result Page Inspiration Prompt

## Page Purpose

Show published performance outcomes with clarity, transparency, and calm structure.

## Key Visual Hierarchy

- score summary hero
- objective and subjective breakdown
- grade or pass/fail badge
- answer review or feedback panel
- next-step or navigation actions

## Target Aesthetic

Professional, data-clear, lightly celebratory for completed work but never playful.

## Important Sections And Components

- score hero card
- metric breakdown row
- tabbed section details
- feedback notes for subjective review

## Interaction Feel

Informative and reflective, not urgent.

## Layout Constraints

- emphasize score without overwhelming details
- use tabs or sections to keep detail organized

## Consistency Rules From `design.md`

- teal for positive completion states
- amber for pending review if shown
- calm charts or metric cards
- stable card pattern matching dashboards
