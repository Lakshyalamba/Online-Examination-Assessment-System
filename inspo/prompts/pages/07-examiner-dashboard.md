# Examiner Dashboard Inspiration Prompt

## Page Purpose

Give examiners a productive overview of authored exams, pending reviews, and question bank health.

## Key Visual Hierarchy

- workload summary cards
- pending review queue panel
- upcoming exams list
- question bank and result analytics snapshot

## Target Aesthetic

Operational and information-dense, while still calm and structured.

## Important Sections And Components

- role-aware sidebar
- summary cards for drafts, scheduled exams, pending reviews, published results
- table or list for upcoming exams
- chart or small analytics row

## Interaction Feel

Actionable and efficient, with quick paths into authoring and grading.

## Layout Constraints

- keep the dashboard denser than student pages
- actions should stay easy to find at the card or header level

## Consistency Rules From `design.md`

- use stable card headers and action zones
- mix metric cards with operational tables
- use muted chart colors with teal highlights
